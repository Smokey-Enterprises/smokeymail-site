About SmokeyIT

Born out of the Great Smoky Mountains region, SmokeyIT was founded to provide small and medium-sized businesses with enterprise-grade cloud technology without the bloated enterprise price tags. We help businesses modernize, automate, and scale their operations seamlessly.

As a specialized solution partner, we partner directly with Zoho to deploy powerful, integrated cloud ecosystems. From high-deliverability custom email setups and automated CRM pipelines to custom helpdesk ticketing (Zoho Desk) and full-suite cloud operating systems (Zoho One), we eliminate technology headaches so you can focus on growing your core business.

We believe in straightforward pricing, personal local support, and zero fluff. Whether you need a simple email migration or a total digital overhaul, SmokeyIT delivers rock-solid IT architecture backed by secure, world-class cloud infrastructure.
